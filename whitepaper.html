Ziran2 White Paper 1.0

Ziran1 is the public announcement layer. Ziran2 is the operational Pair Coin system.

Supply

Genesis and maximum initial supply: 1,000,000,000 ZIRAN. No additional issuance endpoint is included.

Accounts

Email login, salted PBKDF2 password hashes, wallet, Pair ID, and KV session.

Ledger

Each transfer is recorded as one transaction with a debit and credit entry.

Pair Proof

Version 1.0 uses a verifiable SHA-256 relationship over transaction ID, sender Pair, receiver Pair, amount, and time. It is an engineering prototype, not yet a formally proven post-quantum system.
